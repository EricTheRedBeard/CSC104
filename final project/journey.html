MY JOURNEY

The roadmap of my growth into cybersecurity

1996 - MSDOS

The Foundation of coding

My mother got a Tandy computer and an at home course for coding in MSDOS. After learning how to code everything in the course my brother and I utilized the skills to make our own games and versions of the visual elements therein.

1999 - Computer class

School level learning

Welcome to my high school years and the start of being able to take the computer lab class. Because I have been learning on my own and following my brothers learning path I test out of the basic class within a few weeks. The teacher decided that it would be a good idea to see what I could do with HTML coding and gave me a single project for the year, create a school website. In January of 2000 we went live with the site and I maintained it until 2009 when the school put this task on the computer and coding teacher.

2012 - 2024 I walk alone

Self paced learning

During this time I partook in several of the online courses from sites such as stackskills, devslopes and udemy. While these helped me keep my already learned skills at hand I don't feel I learned much from them in practicality. Most of my learning during this time came from a mentorship with someone who ran a gaming server.

Present Day

Securing my Future

Currently in the process of earning my B.S. in Computer Sciences with a specific focus on cybersecurity, more specifically network security. During my studies at UAT (University of Advancing Technology) I am also studying for certifications, currently for my Network+ and Security+ with ambitions to later get my Linux+, CCNA, CEH, CISA, CISM and many more!
